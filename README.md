# Sheffield Cancer Bioinformatics Group

Welcome to the **Sheffield Cancer Bioinformatics Group** website, the online hub for our research group. 

## Explore Our Work

You can explore more about our work by visiting the website at: [sheffieldcancerbioinformatics.github.io](https://sheffieldcancerbioinformatics.github.io)

This site showcases our research projects, publications, and resources. 

For a comprehensive look at our ongoing projects, open-source repositories, and collaborative efforts, check out our [GitHub Organization Page](https://github.com/sheffieldcancerbioinformatics).

## How to Contribute

We welcome contributions to improve our website or the tools and resources we share. If you're interested in contributing, please visit our GitHub organization to find more details about individual projects and follow the contribution guidelines specific to each repository.

## Acknowledgements

Thanks to all members of the research group for their ongoing contributions and dedication to advancing cancer and bioinformatics research. We are also grateful to the broader bioinformatics and cancer research communities for their support and collaboration.
