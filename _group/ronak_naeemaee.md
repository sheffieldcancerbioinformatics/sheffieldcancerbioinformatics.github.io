Ronak is currently pursuing a PhD entitled "*Advanced Survival Analysis Tools for Cancer Biomarker Validation*" under the supervision of <a href="https://www.shu.ac.uk/about-us/our-people/staff-profiles/lewis-quayle" target="_blank">Dr. Lewis Quayle (Director of Studies)</a>, <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/keith-harris-b4363766/" target="_blank">Dr. Keith Harris</a>, <a href="https://www.shu.ac.uk/about-us/our-people/staff-profiles/neil-cross" target="_blank">Dr. Neil Cross</a> and <a href="https://www.sheffield.ac.uk/smph/people/clinical-medicine/jonathan-griffin" target="_blank">Dr Jon Griffin</a> with a focus on developing innovative methods to enhance precision medicine in oncology.

## Professional Experience

### **PhD Candidate | Sheffield Hallam University**
*September 2023 – Present*

- PhD project entitled "*Unlocking Precision Medicine: Advanced Survival Analysis Tools for Cancer Biomarker Validation*".
- Focussed on developing a novel multivariate proportional hazards-based method to assess biomarker-survival relationships, integrating clinical variables for enhanced predictive accuracy in cancer research.
- Aiming to produce an innovative R package and web portal for biomarker validation to make sophisticated analysis techniques accessible to researchers and clinicians, thereby contributing to more personalised and effective cancer treatment strategies.
