We are pleased to launch the website for the Sheffield Cancer Bioinformatics Group.

The development of the site has been some time in the planning, and marks an important step as the group continues to establish and grow. Over the coming year, we anticipate significant expansion of the team and its research activities.

This website provides an overview of our research programmes, collaborative activities, and ongoing work. It also reflects our commitment to open science, with a focus on sharing analytical approaches, tools, and insights that support the wider cancer research community.

We hope the site serves as a useful resource for collaborators, researchers, and anyone interested in our work, and we look forward to sharing updates as the group develops.
